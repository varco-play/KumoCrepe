Our Story

More Than
Just A Crepe

Kumo — the Japanese word for "cloud" — was born from a dream to bring something light, delicate, and soulful to San Marcos. Our crepes are crafted with French technique, local ingredients, and a whole lot of heart.

Whether you're here for a sweet Nutella fold, a savory breakfast wrap, or one of our signature specialty drinks, every bite and sip is made to be a moment worth savoring.
